THE ULTIMATE CHALLENGE

Escape the Grid is more than just a game it’s a challenge of spatial awareness and strategy. Explore, battle, and complete tasks as you fight your way out of the grid. Navigate through the mazes set across five unique worlds: Meadow, Candy, Sky, Lavender, and Lemon.

GAME MODES

Escape Mode

Find the hidden exit buried deep within the grid. Stealth and speed are your best allies.

Battle Mode

Fight for space against relentless opponents. Outmaneuver and outlast in this high-intensity arena.

Quest Mode

Collect rare artifacts and complete legendary quests. Every star gathered brings you closer to mastery.

DYNAMIC MAPS

Break away from the standard box. Experience mazes in various shapes including Cross, T-Shape, H-Shape, L-Shape, E-Shape, U-Shape, and C-Shape. Each map layout presents unique tactical challenges.
